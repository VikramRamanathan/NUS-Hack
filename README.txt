The goal is to help blind people, as many conviences we enjoy, such as information about the world, are unreackable for them.
This application seeks to ease one aspect of their lives, commuting, by giving them access to the bus stop timing so as not to miss their busses,
and also to let them know when they are approaching their destination, so as to not get off at the wrong stop. In the future, this programm 
will also have functionality for basic directions for walking. It will also be on ios and android.
